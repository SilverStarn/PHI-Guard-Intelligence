# Demo Script

## Story

Northstar Family Clinic wants to understand where synthetic patient data lives, how it moves, who can access it, and what to fix first.

## Flow

1. Open the Import tab.
2. Keep the Northstar demo scan or upload mock CSV, JSON, XML, SQLite, SQL, log/text, or ZIP project files.
3. Open the workspace dashboard.
4. Confirm the latest scan and high-risk counts.
5. Open Data Map.
6. Click an identifier-heavy table or finding node.
7. Follow downstream flows into analytics, report, export, or AI-like assets.
8. Open the linked finding.
9. Review evidence, why it matters, blast radius, and remediation.
10. Open Remediation.
11. Review owner, due window, effort, and risk-reduction queue.
12. Open De-identification Readiness.
13. Confirm blockers such as email, DOB/date, phone, MRN, SSN, ZIP, IP, and free text.
14. Open Access Explorer.
15. Export the report preview.

## Closing Message

PHI Guard Intelligence does not say "this is a HIPAA violation." It says "this is a potential privacy or security risk, here is the evidence, here is the likely blast radius, and here is the first remediation move."

Upload mode is for mock or local development data. Do not upload real PHI to the public demo or local portfolio app.
